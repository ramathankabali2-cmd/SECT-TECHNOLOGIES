About Amanah

Understanding our responsibility as caretakers of Allah's creation.

What is Amanah?

In Islam, an Amanah is a trust or responsibility placed upon a person. Allah has entrusted humanity with the Earth and everything within it. As caretakers (Khalifah), we are expected to use natural resources wisely, avoid waste, protect wildlife and preserve the environment for future generations.

Protecting the environment is therefore not only a social responsibility but also an act of worship and obedience to Allah.
