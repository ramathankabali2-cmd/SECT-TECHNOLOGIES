Project Title

Earth: Our Amanah

Environmental Awareness Website Project

Earth is the only planet we have, and it is our responsibility to protect it. Allah has entrusted us with the care of the environment, and it is our duty to fulfill this trust. This website project aims to raise awareness about environmental conservation and inspire action to protect Allah's creation.
