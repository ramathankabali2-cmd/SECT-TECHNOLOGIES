Welcome to Our School

Nateete Muslim High School is committed to providing quality education while nurturing responsible, disciplined and God-conscious learners.

Our school believes that protecting the environment is part of our duty to Allah. Through awareness, practical activities and teamwork, students learn to care for nature and their community.

This website was created to support our NAMSA Day school theme: "Protecting the Environment, A Trust (Amanah) from Allah."

Our Vision

To become a leading day school in both academics and co-curricular activities as well as sound morals.

Our Mission

To produce a holistic child that can handle the challenges of today.

Our Commitment to Environmental Stewardship

At Nateete Muslim High School, we believe that protecting the environment is both a civic duty and an Islamic responsibility. We encourage every learner to become a guardian of Allah's creation through knowledge, discipline and practical action.

Academic Excellence

Students actively participate in academic tournaments, debates and competitions that promote environmental awareness.

School Excellence

Learners are encouraged to excel in academics, sports and co-curricular activities as integral parts of their holistic development.

Environmental Awareness

Students participate in awareness campaigns that inspire responsible environmental practices within the school and community.

Our Core Values

Faith

We strive to live according to Islamic teachings in every aspect of life.

Knowledge

We pursue academic excellence with curiosity, discipline and integrity.

Responsibility

Every student is encouraged to care for the school, community and environment.

Stewardship

We protect Allah's creation through sustainable actions and responsible behaviour.
